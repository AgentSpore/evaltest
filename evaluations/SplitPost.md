## SplitPost Evaluation
- **Overall score:** 9/10
- **Strengths:**
  • Clear value proposition – write once, adapt for many platforms.
  • Strong tech‑stack fit (FastAPI + aiosqlite + OpenRouter).
  • Well‑defined monetisation (freemium → Pro $19/mo).
  • Good market sizing (TAM $18B, SAM $2.1B, SOM $12M).
- **Weaknesses / Risks:**
  • Dependence on third‑party LLM (OpenRouter) – cost & latency considerations.
  • Need for ongoing platform‑specific rule updates as Twitter/X, LinkedIn, etc. change their APIs.
  • Competitive landscape (existing social‑media schedulers).
- **Recommendation:**
  Proceed with a focused MVP that validates the core adaptation loop on two platforms (Twitter/X and LinkedIn) before expanding; negotiate a fixed‑price or volume‑discounted LLM contract to keep operating costs predictable.
