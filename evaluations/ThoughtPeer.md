## ThoughtPeer Evaluation
- **Overall score:** 8/10
- **Strengths:**
  • Strong privacy focus – local LLM analysis, no raw text leaves device.
  • Valuable community insight – anonymous matching helps users feel less alone.
  • Simple tech stack (Python, FastAPI, SQLite, vanilla JS) easy to maintain.
  • Clear monetization path – premium tiers for advanced analytics.
- **Weaknesses / Risks:**
  • Dependence on quality of local LLM; may need model updates.
  • Niche market (journaling/mental health) – user acquisition may be challenging.
  • Potential regulatory considerations around health data.
- **Recommendation:**
  Launch MVP with core local analysis and anonymous matching; partner with mental‑health influencers for early adoption; consider integrating with existing journaling apps for distribution.
