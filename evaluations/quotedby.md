## quotedby Evaluation
- **Overall score:** 7/10
- **Strengths:**
  • Addresses emerging need for LLM visibility/GEO/AEO analytics.
  • Clear monetization via subscription tiers for startups and enterprises.
  • Tech stack (Python, FastAPI, aiosqlite, OpenRouter) allows quick integration of new models.
  • Provides actionable visibility score and competitive benchmarking.
- **Weaknesses / Risks:**
  • Dependence on third‑party LLM APIs – rate limits, cost, and potential changes in model behavior.
  • Market still nascent; educating customers on GEO/AEO may require effort.
  • Possible abuse or manipulation of LLM outputs.
- **Recommendation:**
  Start with a minimal viable tracker for a few popular LLMs (GPT‑4, Claude, Gemini) and offer a free tier to gather feedback; iterate on analytics dashboards and consider partnerships with SEO platforms.
